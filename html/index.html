A little about myself...

Hello World! I'm Anurag, a self-taught UI/UX Designer based in India. I design aesthetically pleasing, user-friendly interfaces for digital products with passion and attention to detail. I design interfaces with an intention of providing users with a meaningful and seamless experience that can help solve their problems while also assisting businesses in achieving their goals.
I spend much of my time learning and honing my design abilities, as well as keeping up with the newest trends and technology. I'm also anxious to explore and learn new things that can improve my design skills and make me a better designer.
